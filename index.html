Ask About Plans

How long does a new website take?

1 to 2 weeks on average, but it depends on the project.

Do I need to provide content and images?

Not required — but you can provide any content whether that be text, images, logos, etc. that you want included.

What platform do you build on?

Hard-coded HTML, CSS & JS, Claude Code, Framer, Shopify, and more depending on what fits your project best.

Will I be able to update my own site?

Most sites need a complete rebuild and the current site isn't worth touching — but you can absolutely transfer your own domain. Otherwise I'll help you get a new one and link it to the new site.

What if I don't love the design?

I build you a demo site. If you don't like it — no harm no foul. If you do, we move forward.

Do you offer payment plans?

50% upfront once you approve the project, and 50% once the final site is delivered.

Do you only work with certain industries?

Not necessarily. Send us a message below and we will reach out!

What happens after launch?

The website is yours. Your domain is yours. If you choose, you can have me manage it for a small monthly fee. Send us a message below and we can figure out what works best for your business!
