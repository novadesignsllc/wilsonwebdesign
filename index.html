Ask About Plans

How long does a new website take?

Most projects are completed in 2–4 weeks depending on scope. Quick fixes can often be done within days. We give you a firm timeline upfront.

Do I need to provide content and images?

Nope — we can write copy and source professional images for you. If you have your own brand assets, even better. We make it easy either way.

What platform do you build on?

We primarily work with WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, and custom HTML/CSS. We recommend the best platform for your specific needs and budget.

Will I be able to update my own site?

Yes. We build on platforms with easy content management and provide a walkthrough so you can make simple updates without needing us.

What if I don't love the design?

All packages include revision rounds. We work with you until you're happy. We've never launched a site a client wasn't proud of.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes — we typically split payment 50% upfront and 50% on completion. We can discuss other arrangements for larger projects.

Do you only work with certain industries?

We work with all types of small businesses — restaurants, contractors, salons, law firms, retail, e-commerce, consultants, and more.

What happens after launch?

Every package includes post-launch support. We also offer ongoing monthly maintenance plans so your site stays fast, secure, and updated.
